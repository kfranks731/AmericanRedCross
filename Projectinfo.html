How long does the AmericanRedCross stay after a disaster strikes?

The AmericanRedCross has to be responsible for helping people clean up after a disaster and find them a shelter.

They are the best people

The AmericanRedCross is the world's best known natural disaster response team. They all work together to help the people that need it most.

People count on them to help them get back on their feet.
